Our Story

Built on Trust

AV Group was founded with a single, clear purpose: to provide businesses, institutions, and individuals with security and facility services they can genuinely depend on — every hour of every day.

Based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, we have grown from a focused security services provider into a full-spectrum facility and manpower solutions company. Our portfolio spans security guards, trained bouncers, VIP protection, CCTV installation, housekeeping, HR & manpower, intelligence, and construction services.

What sets us apart is the quality of our people. Every AV Group personnel undergoes strict background verification, physical fitness assessment, and professional training before deployment. Many of our senior security staff are ex-army, ex-police, or from paramilitary backgrounds — bringing real-world operational experience to every assignment.

We are fully compliant with the Private Security Agencies Regulation Act (PSARA) and all applicable government regulations. Our operations are transparent, accountable, and client-focused at every level.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, professional, and cost-effective security and facility services that protect lives, assets, and business continuity for every client we serve — without exception.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most trusted and professional security and facility management company in Maharashtra — expanding across India — known for integrity and excellence.
