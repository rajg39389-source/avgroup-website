Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can AV Group deploy security personnel?▾

For standard deployments we require 24–48 hours notice to deploy fully vetted and trained personnel. For emergency situations we can arrange deployment within a few hours. Contact us directly on WhatsApp or phone for urgent requirements.

Are your security guards licensed and trained?▾

Yes. All AV Group security personnel are background-verified, physically fit, and trained in security protocols, first aid, communication, and emergency response. Our operations are PSARA compliant and all armed personnel carry valid firearms licenses.

Do you provide security for one-time events?▾

Absolutely. We provide event security for one-day corporate events, concerts, weddings, private parties, exhibitions, and more. We can deploy bouncers, crowd control officers, VIP protection, and event coordination security for any scale of event.

What areas does AV Group cover in Mumbai?▾

We serve all areas of Mumbai including Andheri, Bandra, Borivali, Dadar, Goregaon, Kurla, Lower Parel, Malad, Mulund, Powai, Worli, and the greater Mumbai metropolitan region including Navi Mumbai, Thane, Vasai, and Virar.

How are your services priced?▾

Pricing depends on the type of service, number of personnel, shift timings, location, and contract duration. We provide customized, competitive quotes for every client. Contact us with your requirements and we'll send a detailed quote with no obligation.

Can I hire a personal bouncer for my own protection?▾

Yes. We provide dedicated personal protection officers for individuals — business executives, public figures, or anyone requiring close personal security for daily routine, specific occasions, or travel within Mumbai.

Do you also provide housekeeping along with security?▾

Yes! AV Group offers integrated facility management combining security, housekeeping, CCTV monitoring, and manpower services under one contract. This simplifies management and reduces cost for your business.

Can I book a serviceman directly online?▾

Yes! You can browse our available security guards, bouncers, and other servicemen online and book directly. Simply create a free account on our website, browse the available personnel, select your preferred serviceman, and fill in your booking details. Admin will confirm within 2–4 hours.
